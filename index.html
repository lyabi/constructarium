Selected Contrast Pair

Transitive Construction

He sliced the bread.

Caused Motion Construction

Pat sliced the carrots into the salad.

Contrast Type

transitive vs. caused motion

Explanation

Same verb; transitive encodes simple action on a Patient with no specified endpoint; caused-motion adds a Goal PP, encoding the Theme's change of location as constructionally entailed.
