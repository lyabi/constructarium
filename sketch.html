About

About this project

Constructarium is an interactive reference tool for exploring English grammatical constructions in the framework of Construction Grammar (CxG).

The tool is aimed at linguistics students, advanced English learners, and instructors who want to explore how meaning emerges from grammatical form — not from the lexicon alone.

Data is drawn from foundational CxG literature (Goldberg 1995, 2005, 2009; Kay & Fillmore 1999; Jackendoff 1997, and others) and corpus examples from COCA.
