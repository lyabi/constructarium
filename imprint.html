About This Project

Constructarium was created as part of the course "Web-Entwicklung (Agentic Coding. LLM-gestützte Webentwicklung in der Praxis)" at the University of Graz, at the Department of Digital Humanities, in the summer semester 2026.

The project is a student work and is intended for academic and educational purposes only. It is not a commercial offer.

The technical implementation was developed with the assistance of Claude Code (Anthropic) and Codex (OpenAI).
